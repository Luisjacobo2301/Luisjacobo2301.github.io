Engineering Projects

A collection of research-driven and technical projects across additive manufacturing, finite element analysis, structural mechanics, aerospace systems, and materials testing.

CURIE-S / NASA L’SPACE

Led materials, manufacturing, and design work for a NASA-funded proposal focused on a 3D-printable lunar-regolith-based material system.

NASA L’SPACE Materials Research Siemens NX

PEM Fuel Cell Catalyst Materials

Designed and synthesized polyoxometalate-doped carbon catalyst materials for PEM fuel cells, with characterization using electrochemical and physicochemical methods.

Electrochemistry Synthesis Characterization

3D Printed Aircraft Competition

Helped design a one-meter-wingspan PLA aircraft for a national 3D-printed aircraft competition, applying CAD, 3D printing, and aircraft design principles.

3D Printing Aircraft Design CAD

FDM Structural Analysis

Developed a Python/FEA-based method to predict FDM delamination from g-code using NASMAT and the Generalized Method of Cells.

FEA Python NASMAT

Fuselage FEA Analysis

Modeled a commercial aircraft fuselage section in Abaqus CAE, simulated loading conditions, and prepared a structural analysis and optimization report.

Abaqus CAE FEA Structural Analysis

Mechanical Properties of PLA

Tested tensile strength, flexural modulus, and impact yield of 3D-printed PLA across different print speeds and orientations under ASTM and ISO 17025 guidelines.

Materials Testing ASTM ISO 17025
