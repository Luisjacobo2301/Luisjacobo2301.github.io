POM-doped Graphdiyne PEMFCs

April 2024 - Present Materials Research

Project Overview

Research focused on developing novel catalyst materials for Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFCs) using Polyoxometalate-doped Graphdiyne. This project aims to enhance the efficiency and durability of fuel cells through advanced materials engineering and electrochemical optimization.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Design and synthesis of novel catalyst materials
  • Conduct electrochemical testing and characterization
  • Analyze material performance and stability
  • Document research findings and optimize synthesis procedures

Technical Details

Research Methods

  • Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS)
  • Cyclic Voltammetry (CV)
  • Material Synthesis and Characterization
  • Performance Testing under Various Conditions

Equipment & Technologies

  • Potentiostat/Galvanostat Systems
  • Materials Characterization Tools
  • Data Analysis Software
  • Custom Testing Apparatus
