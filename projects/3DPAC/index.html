3D Printed Aircraft Competition

March 2024 - April 2024 National Competition

Project Overview

Led a team in designing and manufacturing a propeller-driven lightweight aircraft with a 1-meter wingspan for a national competition. The project focused on optimizing aerodynamic performance while ensuring structural integrity using PLA material and advanced 3D printing techniques.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Designed aircraft components using CAD software for optimal aerodynamic performance
  • Developed and implemented 3D printing parameters for structural components
  • Conducted structural analysis and weight optimization
  • Led testing and validation of flight characteristics
